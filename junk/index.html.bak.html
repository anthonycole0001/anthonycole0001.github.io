Biography:

Anthony is a transplant to Boston having moved here in 2002. He has had brief stints away from Boston, but has always returned because it's now considered home. He holds a BA in Behavioral and Social Sciences from the University of Maryland College Park. He is a product manager interested in mobile application development and is currently working on his Masters in Software Engineering at Harvard Extension School, a division of Harvard University. As one of the participants at Startup Institute, Anthony is studying the user centric product design and iterative feature development.

Interests:

Anthony is an avid follower of all things related to entrepreneurship. He previously cofounded an enterprise startup called Cogent Mobile and in his spare time enjoys exploring consumer big data analytics. He has a broad interest in user experience design of various technologies spanning Quantified self, Big Data, SaaS, and consumer analytics. Anthony's previous application development projects have used PHP, ASP.net, JAVA, JSON, REST APIs, XML, SQL and objective-c.
